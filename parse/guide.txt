A: Activated (brown) ability
P: on play
O: once between turns

Action names
	P: play bird
		Px: played bird in zone
	F: gain food
		Fx: if they gain x
	L: lay eggs
	D: draw cards

C - cache
	C?: optionally cache
	Cx: cache an x
H - hunt
	Hx: hunt for <x bird => tuck
T - tuck
	Td: tuck from deck
B - gain from birdfeeder
	Bx: gain x if available
	Bxa: gain all x from birdfeeder
G - gain from supply

P - play second bird in same zone
O - draw 2 bonus cards; keep 1
D - draw card
	Dx: draw x cards
	Dt: draw card from tray
R - repeat action in same row
	Rx: repeat type of power (Rt/Rh)
	(default: repeat brown power)
E - lay an egg
	Ex: on a nest
		Exe: on each nest
	Ex~: on another
	Ex!: on any
M - if rightmost, move
r - roll outside dice
	rx: if rolled an x
W - players with the fewest x birds
A - all players, starting with player of your choice
t - trade (A for A)
- - remove

D - tuck
E - tuck from deck
F - gain from birdfeeder
G - gain all from birdfeeder
H - gain from supply
I - play second bird
J - bonus
K - draw card
L - draw tray card
M - repeat brown power
N - repeat hunter power
O - lay an egg on this bird
P - lay an egg on another bird
Q - lay an egg on any bird

S - move if rightmost
T - roll outside dice
U - players with the fewest
V - all players, starting with player of your choice
W - trade A for A
X - discard (card)
Y - discard an egg from this bird
Z - discard an egg from another bird
a - discard at the end of your turn
b - discard (food)
c - lay egg (action)
d - discard an egg from any bird
e - plays a bird (action)
f - gains a food (action)
g - successful hunt (action)
